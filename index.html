Frequently asked questions

The short version. For everything else, visit our support page.

Do my friends need to download CheckMate too?
Nope. Anyone you invite can view the bill, claim their items, and pay their share right in their phone's browser — no account and no app download required.
Is CheckMate free to use?
Yes. Creating bills, splitting expenses, tracking balances, and sending reminders are all free with no limits. Premium unlocks convenience features like Vacation Mode and advanced spending insights — it never gates the core experience.
Does CheckMate process my payments?
No. CheckMate tracks who owes what and hands you off to the payment app you already use — Venmo, Zelle, or Apple Pay — to actually move the money. We never touch your funds or payment credentials.
How accurate is the receipt scanning?
Very, for most printed receipts — but no OCR is perfect on every faded gas-station printout. You can always edit any item, price, tax, or tip after scanning, before anything is split.
What happens if someone doesn't pay?
CheckMate keeps their share marked as pending and can send them an automatic reminder. It's still on you and your friends to actually settle up — CheckMate just makes it impossible to lose track of who owes what.
Which countries and currencies are supported?
CheckMate works anywhere Venmo, Zelle, or Apple Pay are used for the payment step, and Vacation Mode supports automatic conversion across major world currencies for splitting the bill itself.
